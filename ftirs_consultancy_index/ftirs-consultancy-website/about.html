About FTIRS Consultancy

Your student-first education consultancy in Islamabad

We provide structured, transparent and practical guidance for international admissions, scholarships and study visa preparation.

Our Story

FTIRS Consultancy was created to simplify study abroad planning for Pakistani students. Our focus is to help every student understand real options, avoid documentation mistakes and apply with confidence.

Instead of one-size-fits-all advice, we review your academic record, study gap, budget, language test status and future goals before recommending a destination or university.

🎓

Mission

To connect students with suitable international education pathways through ethical counselling and complete application support.

🌍

Vision

To become a trusted name for study abroad guidance in Islamabad and across Pakistan.

🤝

Values

Transparency, accuracy, quick communication, confidentiality and student success.
